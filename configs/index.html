What?

Xonotic is a free and opensource game that is based on Quake.

How?

Download

Click here to download the zip file.
Just unzip it and launch the correct version for your operating system.

Connect

Once you launch the application, click on Multiplayer at the top right.
Then punch in xonotic.codyhill.co the address field and click join!
